Where AI projects stall

Hi {{ contact.FIRSTNAME|default:"there" }},

Most AI projects do not stall because the software is incapable. They stall because the business is not ready to give the software a clean job.

These are the four gaps I would look for first:

Scattered instructions. The process lives in one person's memory, old documents, and chat threads.
Unreliable inputs. Customer, project, or sales data is incomplete or entered differently across systems.
Disconnected tools. People still copy and paste information between inboxes, spreadsheets, and core applications.
No clear owner. Nobody is responsible for monitoring exceptions, correcting errors, or improving the workflow.

That is not bad news. It is a useful diagnosis. A short cleanup phase often creates more value than rushing into another software subscription.

The right starting point is usually the workflow with a visible problem, usable data, and an owner who wants it fixed.
