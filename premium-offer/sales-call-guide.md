# CRS365 AI Automation Blueprint Sales Call Guide

## Call objective

Decide whether one clearly defined workflow is a good fit for the $995 Blueprint. Do not diagnose the entire company or design the solution during the sales call.

## Fit criteria

A strong fit has:

- One recurring workflow with a visible problem
- A person who owns the process
- Access to current process information and representative examples
- Willingness to keep human approval where risk requires it
- Authority to purchase or a clear approval path
- Interest in a practical 30-day plan rather than a broad AI presentation

The Blueprint is not a fit when the prospect wants immediate implementation, unlimited workflow coverage, custom software development, a cybersecurity or legal opinion, vendor procurement, or guaranteed savings.
