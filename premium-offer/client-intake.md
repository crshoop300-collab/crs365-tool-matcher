# CRS365 AI Automation Blueprint Intake

Use this intake after payment and before the 60-minute kickoff. Request only the materials needed for the agreed workflow.

## Business context

1. Organization name
2. Primary contact name, role, and email
3. Industry and approximate team size
4. Which business function owns this workflow?
5. What business outcome should this Blueprint support?
6. Why is this workflow a priority now?

## Workflow scope

1. Give the workflow a short name.
2. What event starts the workflow?
3. What information must be available before work begins?
4. Describe the current steps from start to finish.
5. Who performs or approves each major step?
6. What output marks the workflow as complete?
7. Who receives that output?
8. How often does the workflow run?
9. Approximately how much staff time does one cycle require?
10. Where do delays, errors, rework, or missed handoffs occur?
11. What exceptions require judgment?
12. Which steps must remain human-controlled?

## Tools and data

1. List the applications used during this workflow.
2. Where is the system of record?
3. Which tools already exchange data?
4. Where do people copy and paste information manually?
5. What reports or dashboards currently exist?
6. Are APIs, webhooks, exports, or administrator access available?
7. Are any tools scheduled to be replaced?
8. Attach redacted screenshots, forms, SOPs, reports, or sample outputs that show how the workflow operates.

## Risk and governance

1. Does the workflow involve confidential, financial, health, employment, customer, or regulated information?
2. Are there retention, approval, audit, or access-control requirements?
3. Which decisions cannot be delegated to AI or automation?
4. Who should review exceptions and errors?
5. What would make an automated outcome unacceptable?

## Success and implementation

1. What should improve: time, accuracy, response speed, visibility, capacity, consistency, or another measure?
2. What baseline data is available today?
3. Who will own the improved workflow?
4. Who must approve implementation?
5. What budget range is available for software or implementation?
6. Are there deadlines, seasonal constraints, or other projects that affect timing?
7. What would make the first 30 days a clear success?

## Required kickoff materials

- Completed intake
- Current workflow notes or SOP
- List of current tools and owners
- Two or three representative examples of inputs and outputs, with sensitive data removed
- Available baseline metrics
- Names of the workflow owner and required approvers
