# CRS365 AI Automation Blueprint

The CRS365 AI Automation Blueprint is the fixed-scope premium offer that follows the free AI Fit Score.

## Offer summary

- Price: $995 fixed fee
- Scope: one priority workflow or operating process
- Turnaround: five business days after the kickoff and receipt of required materials
- Included meetings: 60-minute kickoff and 45-minute findings review
- Included revision: one clarification revision requested within seven days of delivery
- Primary CTA: book a 30-minute Fit Score review
- Booking URL: https://calendly.com/chadshoop/30-minute-consult
- Proposed public page: https://crs365.com/ai-automation-blueprint/

## Customer outcome

The client receives a decision-ready plan that explains what to automate, how the workflow should operate, where human approval remains necessary, which tools should be used, how success will be measured, and what to implement during the first 30 days.

## Included

1. Business objective and workflow scope
2. Current-state workflow and friction analysis
3. AI, data, and systems readiness scorecard
4. Future-state workflow design
5. Tool, integration, and data recommendations
6. Human approval, risk, and exception controls
7. 30-day implementation roadmap
8. Success metrics and ownership plan
9. Editable Word Blueprint and findings review

## Boundaries

- One workflow or business process
- Up to five core applications reviewed in depth
- No software configuration, custom development, data migration, legal review, cybersecurity audit, or implementation included
- Additional workflows and implementation are quoted separately
- Recommendations are based on the information available during the engagement and do not guarantee financial or operational outcomes

## Offer ladder

Review pricing and scope after the first five paid Blueprint clients. Track delivery time, revision load, implementation conversion, and client-reported value before changing the fee.

1. Push this repository so the public report images are available at fitscore.crs365.com.
2. Create a WordPress page with the slug ai-automation-blueprint.
3. Choose the site's full-width page template and hide the default page title and sidebar.
4. Paste ai-automation-blueprint-wordpress-block.html into one WordPress Custom HTML block.
5. Publish and verify the hero image, report preview, Calendly links, privacy link, terms link, desktop layout, and mobile layout.
6. Only after the page is live, update the Fit Score and nurture emails to link to the public offer page.

## Purchase and delivery flow

1. Prospect books the free 30-minute Blueprint fit call.
2. CRS365 confirms one qualified workflow and the scope boundaries.
3. CRS365 sends the fixed-fee agreement and $995 payment request.
4. After payment, the client receives the intake and 60-minute kickoff link.
5. The five-business-day delivery clock starts after the kickoff and receipt of required materials.
6. CRS365 delivers the editable Blueprint and conducts the 45-minute findings review.
7. One clarification revision may be requested within seven days.
8. Implementation is offered only through a separate scope and quote.

No payment link is hard-coded into the sales page. This prevents a prospect from purchasing an engagement before CRS365 confirms that the workflow fits the fixed scope.
